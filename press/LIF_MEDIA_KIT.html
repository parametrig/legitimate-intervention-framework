🎯 The Framework

LIF proposes a 2-dimensional taxonomy for classifying interventions:

Scope (Precision): Network → Asset → Protocol → Module → Account

Authority (Legitimacy): Signer Set → Delegated Body → Governance

The "Optimistic Freeze" model: Emergency Council acts immediately under pre-approved rules; DAO ratifies within 24-48h or action lapses.
